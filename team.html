Our Team

At Medincas we believe, teamwork is the collaborative effort of a group to accomplish a task in the most efficient and effective way. As part of a team, we are a group of interdependent individuals working towards a common objective, this concept is seen within the larger framework.

Creative Team

Our team consists of highly skilled and creative 3D artists, designers, animators, illustrators, and developers with extensive clinical experience. As medical scientists and factual broadcast producers, our graphics and scientific accuracy are of feature-film quality. Multiple global healthcare awards and international broadcast features recognize our medical and scientific expertise, creativity, and standards of excellence.

Scientific and Medical Team

Throughout the discovery and post-commercialization stages of multiple therapies, our scientific and medical experts have extensive experience. The scientific experts on our creative team have strong pharmacological backgrounds, including BSc and PhD degrees. They are also well-versed in legal, medical, and approval processes.

Tech Team

A tech team develops the technology needed to produce visual effects. They create systems that technical directors can utilize and modify as needed. They also design new digital tools and ensure they fit into existing software systems. This makes it possible to pass assets efficiently from one VFX process to another. Additionally, this team works on research and development, which means it seeks ways to improve digital processes.

We believe in Simple, Creative & Flexible Design Standards.
